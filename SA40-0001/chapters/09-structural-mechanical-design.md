# Structural and Mechanical Design

The structural integrity of the equipment is checked with respect to the possible modes of failure:
- Yielding
- In accordance to EN1993-1 Eurocode 3.
- Buckling
- Frame/beam structures are in accordance to EN1993-1 Eurocode 3.
- Stiffened and unstiffened plated structures in accordance to EN1993-1 Eurocode 3 or DNV-RP-C201 Buckling strength of plated structures.
- Fatigue
- Generally, fatigue capacity is in accordance to DNV-RP-C203 Fatigue design of offshore steel structures.

The structural integrity assessment of the equipment is based on load cases, load- and resistance-factors in accordance with the equipment specific standards listed in {{tbl:main-refs}}.

The most unfavorable position, direction and combination of loads are considered.

The stiffness of the structure is also checked in order to ensure acceptable displacements and vibrations.
- Mechanical components for lifting and handling equipment appliances are designed according to DNV-ST-0378. Main hoisting draw works are according to API 7K.
- Gears, bearings, seals and other mechanical components, which are generally according to vendor's standard, shall be evaluated and selected by HMH as part of the design process.
- All selection of components shall be the responsibility of HMH.
