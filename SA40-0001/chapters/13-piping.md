# Piping
- Pressure piping within the equipment is designed, built and tested according to ASME B31.3 as the principal piping code.
- Drilling riser piping is designed, built and tested according to relevant API, DNV and NACE standards listed in table 4-1.
- Suction pipes and drain pipes are designed and built according to internal HMH procedures.
- Small bore tubing for fluid power are of metric size, detailed in pipe class sheets developed by HMH. The pressure capacity is designed according to ASME.
- All pressure piping is pressure tested before delivery (not tank return and leak oil piping).
- The piping within the equipment is an intrinsic part of the product design and spare part hierarchy, hence it is not to be customized.
- HMH can supply an optional interface kit to the customer's piping system.
