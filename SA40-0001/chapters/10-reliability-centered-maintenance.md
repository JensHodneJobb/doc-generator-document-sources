# Reliability Centered Maintenance

Through HMH's Reliability Centered Maintenance (RCM) service, customized reliability and maintenance analyses are outlined to define a maintenance management regime focusing on cost-effectiveness and risk reduction. This drives effective proactive- and condition-based maintenance (CBM) and prompts the extension of the renewal classification beyond the normal 5 years interval.

To prepare the equipment for condition based maintenance, a qualitative RCM analysis will be performed as part of our design, and a special maintenance manual will be developed for this purpose. This is an option that is available for critical equipment, as detailed in the particular product specification.

RCM analysis is performed in accordance with the following standards
- SAE JA1011-Evaluation Criteria for RCM Process
- SAE JA1012 A Guide to the Reliability Centered Maintenance (RCM) Standard
- IEC 60300 Dependability Management part 3-11 Application Guide -Reliability Centered Maintenance

Additional services offered by HMH related to implementation and monitoring during operation are beyond the scope of this specification
