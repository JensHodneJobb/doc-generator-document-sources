# CE Marking
- European EU directives/regulations shall apply when mandatory
- Equipment for non-movable installations within the EU/EEA/UK is CE marked by HMH.
- CE marking is not applicable for territories outside the EU/EEA/UK, for seagoing vessels and mobile offshore units. HMH will as normal not CE mark such equipment, but the technical requirements of the directives/regulations may be implemented as an option to any customer upon agreement.
- Upon delivery of the equipment, an EC/EU Declaration of Conformity comprehending all relevant directives and harmonized standards is issued by HMH.
- Technical files and declarations of components are kept by HMH or notified body as mandated by the relevant EU directive.
- Partly complete equipment is not CE marked. A declaration of incorporation is issued as mandated in the relevant EU directive.

The following EU directives are included, when relevant:
- Equipment for explosive atmospheres 2014/34/EU (ATEX)
- Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC (MD)
- Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/ EU (PED)
- Electromagnetic Compatibility Directive 2014/30/EU (EMC)
- Low Voltage Directive 2014/35/EU (LVD)

Note: High pressure manifolds for well control are exempted from the Pressure Equipment Directive.
