# Introduction

This document is a technical specification of HMH drilling products describing the basic quality which is built into the products by default.

This specification is intended to meet the demands that are commonly expected within the offshore industry. However, any and all warranties and/or guarantees in equity or at law are waived.
- This specification shall be read in conjunction with the relevant product specification(s), HMH document code SA04, the general arrangement drawing and HMH's quotation letter, which give additional information.
- Apart from this, there is no specific reference to any other HMH standards. The quality is based on internationally recognized standards which are implemented in HMH's internal procedures and governing documents, to the extent applicable and/or desired.
- This specification can be used as a contract basis without any additional requirements, or as a platform for further negotiations, which can result in a revised offer.
- Principal regulations, as described in chapter 3, must be clearly identified in the contract. Although the technical quality is standardized, and may need only minor amendments, there may be various administrative requirements that have an impact on cost and delivery time (e.g. documentation and approvals).
- Any options mentioned in this document shall be priced separately, and are consequently excluded from the scope of work and price.
- This document will be revised and updated on a regular basis, to ensure the technical quality is compliant with relevant regulations and market demands.
